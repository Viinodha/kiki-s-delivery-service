It's been 𝟹𝟽 years since the release of Kiki's Delivery Service, and I thought there was no better time to create a little tribute to one of my favorite movies. 🎥👏🏼

In the film, Kiki starts her own delivery business, known as the "Witch Delivery Service" (おとどけものいたしますキキ), delivering goods across the city on her broomstick. Throughout her journey, Osono supports and encourages her, and I wanted to help our girl out too—by giving her business an interactive website! :b

🌟 𝗔𝗯𝗼𝘂𝘁 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗽𝗿𝗼𝗷𝗲𝗰𝘁 🌟

This is a fictional delivery service website inspired entirely by Kiki's Delivery Service.

Visitors can choose between two services:

- 🍞 Guchokipanya Bakery Delivery — Order freshly baked goods from the beloved bakery.
- 🧚🏻‍♀️ Kiki's Personal Delivery Service — Have Kiki pick up items from your doorstep and deliver them safely across the city.

• 𝗙𝗲𝗮𝘁𝘂𝗿𝗲𝘀

-  A flying Kiki who follows your cursor and leaves behind a trail of sparks and sakura petals.🌸
-  Soft Ghibli-inspired background music with a toggle button (featuring Jiji enjoying some music 🥹).
-  Mobile-friendly layout, with ongoing improvements for an even smoother responsive experience.
-  A flying broom animation after placing an order.

• 𝗕𝘂𝗶𝗹𝘁 𝗪𝗶𝘁𝗵 👩‍💻

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript

Note:

This project was created for fun, learning, and as a tribute to a film that continues to inspire people around the world. While building it, I learned a lot about web development, problem-solving, and bringing a themed experience to life on the web.
